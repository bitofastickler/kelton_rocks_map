Rocks have been painted in honor of Kelton and placed in locations around the world.
If you find one, leave one, or simply want to indicate how Kelton impacted your life, click “Kelton Rocks!” and give permission to log your location. Each sighting adds an anonymous pin to the memorial map.

Privacy: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or accounts. If you allow location, we store only latitude, longitude, and a timestamp.

Tip: On mobile devices, location permission works best over HTTPS.
